# Product Roadmap

## 1. Roadmap Philosophy

The roadmap prioritizes:

- premium perception
- scalable architecture
- controlled complexity
- mobile-first conversion
- maintainability
- business validation

The platform should evolve intentionally.

The goal is NOT to compete directly with Shopify or large ecommerce ecosystems.

The goal is to dominate a specific positioning:

> premium editorial storefronts for Instagram and WhatsApp-driven brands.

---

# 2. Current Product Stage

Current stage:

> Foundation + Premium Storefront Engine

The product already has:
- Angular architecture
- Firebase integration
- multi-tenant foundation
- product ingestion
- storefront rendering
- premium UI direction
- mobile-first philosophy

The platform is transitioning from:
- frontend project

to:

- scalable SaaS product

---

# 3. Strategic Direction

The business strategy is:

- medium number of customers
- medium ticket
- controlled operational complexity
- premium positioning
- scalable presets
- low support chaos

The platform intentionally avoids:
- unlimited customization
- enterprise ERP complexity
- marketplace behavior
- operational bloat

---

# 4. North Star

The product succeeds when:

- a small brand can launch quickly
- the storefront feels premium
- the storefront feels trustworthy
- the storefront converts mobile traffic
- the brand looks established
- the platform remains scalable

---

# 5. Product Positioning

The platform is positioned between:

- editorial luxury websites
- conversion-focused storefronts
- social-commerce experiences

The platform should feel:
- elegant
- modern
- premium
- curated
- conversion-oriented

The platform should validate manually before scaling aggressively.

1. build strong demos
2. validate real customers
3. refine positioning
4. improve presets
5. standardize operations
6. scale acquisition

Avoid:
- scaling too early
- premature team expansion
- excessive infrastructure spending
