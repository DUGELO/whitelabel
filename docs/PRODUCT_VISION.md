# Product Vision

## 1. Product Definition

This product is a premium multi-tenant storefront platform for small and medium visual brands that sell mainly through Instagram and WhatsApp.

It is not a generic ecommerce builder.

The product helps brands look more authentic, consolidated, elegant and trustworthy through premium storefront experiences.

The main value is not only listing products.  
The main value is increasing perceived brand value.

---

## 2. Positioning

The platform sits between:

- editorial/luxury brand experience
- ecommerce conversion
- mobile-first storefront
- WhatsApp/Instagram sales flow

The goal is to make small brands feel like established brands.

The platform should help the customer’s customer think:

> “This store feels authentic, trustworthy and consolidated.”

---

## 3. Market Direction

The market already has large ecommerce platforms such as Shopify, Nuvemshop and Wix.

This product does not compete by offering more operational complexity.

It competes by solving a specific visual and conversion problem:

- generic stores look amateur
- many small sellers depend on Instagram/WhatsApp
- paid traffic needs a high-converting destination
- mobile experience is critical
- visual trust increases conversion

Brazilian ecommerce and social commerce remain highly relevant, with mobile and WhatsApp playing a strong role in purchase journeys. This supports the decision to focus on mobile-first storefronts connected to WhatsApp and Instagram.  
Sources: U.S. International Trade Administration on Brazil ecommerce growth; Reuters on Latin America mobile ecommerce; Meta on WhatsApp commerce in Brazil.  

---

## 4. Core Differentiation

The platform’s core differentiator is:

> Premium visual storefronts with controlled customization.

Not unlimited customization.  
Not generic templates.  
Not a full Shopify replacement.

The platform offers curated presets that preserve quality, consistency and brand perception.

---

## 5. Target Customers

Primary customers:

- small premium brands
- semi-jewelry stores
- accessories stores
- fashion boutiques
- cosmetics/skincare brands
- visual niche stores selling through Instagram and WhatsApp

Ideal customer profile:

- already sells online informally
- uses Instagram and WhatsApp
- needs to look more professional
- does not want to configure a complex ecommerce platform
- wants something beautiful, fast and ready to sell

---

## 6. Customer Promise

The promise:

> A premium storefront that makes your brand look established and helps turn Instagram/WhatsApp traffic into sales.

Possible positioning phrases:

- “Premium storefronts for brands that sell through Instagram.”
- “Turn your Instagram traffic into a beautiful shopping experience.”
- “A storefront that makes your brand look bigger, sharper and more trustworthy.”
- “Editorial storefronts built for conversion.”

---

## 7. What This Product Is

This product is:

- a premium storefront engine
- a visual commerce layer
- a multi-tenant catalog platform
- a mobile-first brand experience
- a conversion-focused product showcase
- a system of controlled premium presets

---

## 8. What This Product Is Not

This product is not:

- a free website builder
- a generic ecommerce platform
- a drag-and-drop builder
- a marketplace
- an ERP
- a logistics system
- a fully custom agency service
- a clone of Shopify, Wix or Nuvemshop

---

## 9. Business Model Direction

Preferred model:

- setup fee
- monthly subscription

Initial pricing idea:

- setup: R$300 to R$800
- monthly: R$49 to R$149

The product should avoid becoming custom development per client.

Customizations must happen through presets, tokens and configuration.

---

## 10. Strategic Principles

1. Premium perception is the core value.
2. Mobile-first is mandatory.
3. Controlled customization beats unlimited customization.
4. Visual consistency is more important than feature quantity.
5. WhatsApp and Instagram are primary conversion channels.
6. The storefront must feel elegant, authentic and trustworthy.
7. Never solve one client’s request with hardcoded tenant-specific code.
8. Build a platform, not a collection of custom websites.
9. Performance matters because paid traffic is expensive.
10. Every UI decision should increase trust, clarity or conversion.

---

## 11. Product North Star

The product succeeds when a small brand can launch a storefront that feels premium, authentic and conversion-ready without needing to understand ecommerce complexity.

The storefront should make the brand look more professional than its current Instagram-only presence and simpler than a traditional ecommerce setup.
